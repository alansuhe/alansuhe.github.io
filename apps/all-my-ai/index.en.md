**All My AI is a desktop tool that centralizes your AI Agent conversations.**

Ready to use upon installation, no registration required, no personal data collection. All data is stored locally only.

No cloud, no distractions, no binding—pure and direct experience for your peace of mind.

---

### Key Features

- Centralized management of AI platform conversations
- Quick search and navigation to historical sessions
- Multi-platform account switching support
- Independent proxy configuration for each AI Agent
- Hotkey activation for efficient workflow
